The A-Block

The home of English, Science, and the Social Sciences

The A-Block is a block of the school primarily used for English classes, science labs, and the social sciences rooms. It also has an outdoor courtyard in its centre, and a small commonroom that contains microwaves, benches, and has bathrooms off its side. This area is generally marked as an area for senior students, as it is where their classes are centred, and where useful things such as lockers are kept. For junior areas, look at blocks "B" and "D".

A list of the classes in this block include:

A1, A2, A3, A4, A5, A6, A7, A8, A9, A10, A11, A12, A13, A14, A15.

Classes 2-4 are used mostly as english rooms, with english classes also being held in A6 and A11. The science labs are A15 and A13 (with a storage room in the middle being A14) There is a teacher workroom that comes off of the common room, so if you need any help in this area that is a good place to go!
