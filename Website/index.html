To the Cromwell College interactive map

This is a map created to assist new and/or lost students at Cromwell College.

It works by simply clicking onto the part of the map you would like to explore. Once you click on a building you will be taken to its page, which includes common classes and the teachers who usually work there. If you only wish for information, and know which block you are trying to access, feel free to use the navigation bar at the top of the page.

Click any building below for information!

**Whilst in current stage, only blocks A, B, and C are clickable**
